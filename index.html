soas

soas restaurant located in downtown Toronto serves contemporary cuisine focused on seasonal, interesting, and local ingredients. The food and menu are evolved according to what's available and as a reflection of our enviornment and people surrounding around us.

We offer an a la carte dinner menu featuring quality ingredients as well as a tasting menu with bites and flavours not offered or featured on the a la carte menu. For private dining and events please contact our events team.

Toronto born Chef Andrew Wu has cooked and trained in Toronto, Singapore, and Melbourne. Notable restaurants including The Harbord Room, Colborne Lane, The Tippling Club in Singapore, and Estelle in Melbourne.

soas (son of a shoemaker) is his first restaurant, its namesake inspired from Andrew's father and a tongue in cheek nod to the colloquial use of of the term, shoemaker, in restaurants around the world.

shoemaker - noun/colloquialism

A cook or chef that does not put effort into their work. Often thought of as lazy, incompetent, lacking in skill or workmanship.
